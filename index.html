The Taboo Block Party

Sunday 25th September

Taboo in Sandton is the home to some of the biggest parties in Gauteng and is informally known as “The Most Loved Club in Mzansi”. This 2016, we give birth to a different experience housed by the block that Taboo lives in. We take the party out of the club and into the block. On Sunday the 25th of September 2016, 24 Central Building will be home to the 1st ever TABOO BLOCK PARTY. A daytime experience like no other. Taboo will transform the block’s parking into a VIP experience tailored for the young and fresh, the hip and happening. Come experience Taboo in a different space, come experience the 1st ever, Taboo Block Party with live entertainment by some of South Africa’s biggest acts, including DJ Dimplez, Sphe & Naves, MajorLeague, DJ Shimza, Ambitious Ent, Speedsta and many more talented acts. 


The Taboo Block Party promises to be a fun filled daytime into night time experience which will bring new life into the entertainment industry
